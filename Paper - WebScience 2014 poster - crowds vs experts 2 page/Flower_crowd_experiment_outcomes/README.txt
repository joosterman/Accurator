Dataset made available supporting Web Science 2014 conference.
Paper: Crowdsourcing Knowledge Intensive Tasks in Cultural Heritage
Files in dataset
	- dataset_complete.csv		- The collection of 82 prints used in the experiment
	- expert_labels.csv		- The collected labels from the experts
	- f7383708_with_untrusted.csv	- The collected data from crowd workers unfiltered by CrowdFlower
	- workset383708.csv		- The set of users who interacted with the tasks
	- flowercrowds.R		- The R script used to process the data
